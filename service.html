Our Services

Services Built Specifically For Patient Care

Patient Management

This system facilitates seamless patient management by efficiently handling registration, admission, discharge, and transfer processes.

It maintains electronic health records (EHR), ensuring easy access to patient demographics, medical history, and treatment plans. Through unique identifiers, it tracks patient interactions with the healthcare


Appointment Scheduling

With advanced scheduling features, the hospital management system optimizes the allocation of resources and time slots for patient appointments.

Patients can easily book appointments with healthcare providers, specialists, and diagnostic services through user-friendly interfaces, reducing wait times and improving patient satisfaction.


Inventory Management

Efficient inventory management ensures the availability of essential medical supplies, drugs, and equipment, minimizing stockouts and wastage.

The system tracks inventory levels in real-time, generates alerts for low stock or expiring items, and optimizes procurement and storage processes to support uninterrupted patient care


HR Management

Employee Onboarding and Training: The system facilitates the onboarding process for new hires, including electronic forms submission, orientation schedules, and access to training materials.

It tracks employee training and certifications, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements and staff competency standards.


Doctor Management

Provider Credentialing and Privileging: The system manages provider credentials, licenses, and certifications, ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements and accreditation standards.


It streamlines the privileging process, verifying provider qualifications, credentials .Performance tracking and evaluation features may also be included to assess doctors' productivity, patient outcomes, and adherence to clinical protocols.


Profile Management

The system offers a seamless and secure profile management system where patients, healthcare providers, and staff can create and manage their profiles using Aadhaar-based authentication.

Aadhaar authentication provides a robust and reliable identity verification mechanism, enhancing security and reducing the risk of identity theft or fraudulent activities.
